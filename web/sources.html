Earnings & cohort data

AHEAD PPD:2026 — Program Performance Data file 112908

Publisher: U.S. Department of Education / National Center for Education Statistics, distributed via the AHEAD negotiated-rulemaking session-2 materials.

Grain: institution × 4-digit CIP × credential level. Earnings derive from IRS records (W-2 + Schedule SE), measured at the fourth tax year following completion.

What this tool reads from it: md_earn_wne_p4 (median earnings), count_wne_p4 (single-window cohort count), fail_obbb_cip2_wageb (PPD-published verdict flag), cipx (4-digit CIP), credlev.

Verify: Open the file in your spreadsheet or SQL tool; filter to your institution and the (cip4, credlev) of interest.

Methodology documentation: PPD:2026 Technical Data Appendix.

IPEDS Completions Survey (C-survey)

Publisher: NCES, U.S. Department of Education.

Grain: UNITID institution × 6-digit CIP × award level × academic year.

What this tool reads from it: Completer counts at programs that may be invisible to AHEAD's PPD (B16 invisibility) — used by R14 to surface programs that exist at the institution but have no row in PPD.

Verify: IPEDS Data Center.

Statutes

Public Law 119-21 — One Big Beautiful Bill Act (OBBBA)

Section of interest: § 84001 (the program-level Earnings Premium test); § 81001 (graduate-loan caps).

Verify: PLAW-119publ21 PDF on Congress.gov — official enrolled text.

Higher Education Act § 498

Topic: Administrative-capability authority cited by the Department for the proposed § 668.16(t) institution-level escalation pathway.

Verify: U.S. Code via House Office of the Law Revision Counsel — Title 20.

Proposed rule (NPRM)

Publisher: U.S. Department of Education.

What this tool reads from it: Proposed § 668.2(b), § 668.402(c)(3), § 668.403(d)(1), § 668.404(d), § 668.43(d)(1), § 668.603, § 668.16(t); RIA Tables 3.1, 3.9, 3.19; preamble Sections IX.GD (Cohort Period, Earnings Threshold).

Comment period closes 2026-05-20.
